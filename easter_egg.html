Welcome to the easter-egg. I'll tell you a few extra fun facts about me as a reward. My calling card is the ASCII eye, based on the "Sight Stone" from a favorite videogame of mine called "StoneStory RPG."

Stone story is an idle-battler RPG that is free on mobile app stores, and is done entirely in the ASCII artstyle, the whole game is made out of ASCII characters.

Why "Zero?"

"Zero" is a leftover from an ArmA 3 campaign that I participated in back in 2020-2022. It was my "codename" or "callsign" and I have kept it ever since as my online handle. It is representative of both my role within that group as team-leader, and of a desire to be unknown. Zero is both the start of all things, and yet is also considered to be innocuous.
